Scalability:

The ability of a system to handle a growing number of users, transactions, or data volumes without affecting its performance. Scalability is crucial for businesses that expect their website or application to experience a significant increase in traffic over time.

Security:

The protection of information and systems from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, disruption, modification, or destruction. Security is essential for any system that handles sensitive data or financial transactions, and it helps to prevent fraud, data breaches, and other cyber threats.

Usability:

The degree to which a system is easy to learn, use, and navigate. Usability is critical for websites, applications, and other digital systems that are designed for end-users, as it can impact user satisfaction, engagement, and adoption.

Availability:

The extent to which a system or service is operational and accessible to users when needed. Availability is essential for mission-critical systems, such as e-commerce websites or financial services, as any downtime or service interruption can result in significant revenue loss or reputational damage.
